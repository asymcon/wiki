<h1>Ninebot ESx - SNSC 1.x series</h1>

<div class="level1">
<p>The Ninebot ESx series is a very popular line of scooters, due to their use both in retail and rental markets.<br />
The same rough design is carried over the whole line, the scooter always including a mechanical rear foot brake/electrical front brake combo, as well as an external battery port and a display.</p>
